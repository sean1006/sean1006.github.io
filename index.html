But the space whales were not alone. Sharing the Milky Way with them was a nation of time-traveling crabs. These crafty crustaceans were gifted with the ability to manipulate the time-space continuum, moving seamlessly through the past, present, and future. Their advanced technology had earned them a reputation as a force to be reckoned with.
